# Forms and Validation

## Form Components
- Login Form (phone/email, password)
- Register Form (name, phone, email, password, userType, location, address, categories)
- Job Creation Form (description, paymentOffer, location, address)
- Review Form (rating, comment)
- Profile Update Form

## Validation Rules
- Required fields
- Phone number format (+91XXXXXXXXXX)
- Email format
- Password strength (min 6 characters)
- Payment amount (positive number)
- Rating (1-5 stars)
